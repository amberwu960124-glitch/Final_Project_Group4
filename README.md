Senior Social Engagement & Health Care App
1. Project Overview
This project addresses the dual challenges faced by seniors: social isolation and the lack of accessible medical information during emergencies. Our app serves as a seamless tool that integrates a Community Hub for social activity registration and an Emergency Data Shield for critical health information access.

2. Key Features

Community Hub: Instant browsing and one-click registration for local senior activities to boost social inclusion.


Emergency Data Shield: Secure and instant access to health profiles, including drug allergies, for medical first responders.


Senior-Friendly UI: A simple interface designed specifically to reduce the high barrier to entry for elderly users.

3. Target Users & Needs

Elderly Seniors: Need user-friendly interfaces and opportunities for social engagement.


Medical First Responders: Need instant and clear access to seniors' health data and allergy information during emergencies.
